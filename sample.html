As a matter of tradition whenever there is SOPA or State of the Province Address, there is also ALAR or Annual Legislative Accomplishment Report of the Vice Governor. Please fasten your seat belt and please lend me your ears. Ladies and gentlemen, this is my report:

The incumbent Sangguniang Panlalawigan is called “the 19th Sangguniang Panlalawigan”. Our term start last June 30, 2019 until June 30, 2022. We are 15 Members of the August Body. Five (5) Regular Members from the 1st District, five (5) Regular Members from the 2nd District, four (4) Ex-Officio Members and the Vice Governor as Presiding Officer to complete the 15 Members. We conducted Regular Committee Meetings and Sessions every Tuesday of the week aside from the special session as requested by the Honorable Governor and agreed upon by 2/3 of its members.

The Vice Governor’s Office has 29 regular personnel and staff, with 6 newly appointed, 12 coterminus, 71 Contract of Service (COS) and 71 Job Orders (JOs) while the Tanggapan ng Sangguniang Panlalawigan has 102 personnel and staff with 93 regular employees, 16 of which are newly appointed/promoted and 9 JOs. The Sangguniang Panlalawigan Members personnel and staff of the 14 offices of the 14 Sangguniang Panlalawigan Members have a total of 453 staff and personnel.

The following are the sum up or this is a package then not really an Annual Report since we have not conducted a Legislative Report due to COVID-19 so, this is a package report from July 2019 to June 2022.

The Sangguniang Panlalawigan conducted 140 regular sessions and four (4) special sessions, 1,393 Committee and Joint Committee Meetings. We approved and adopted Two Thousand Four Hundred Sixty-Nine (2,469) Resolutions and enacted one hundred one (101) Ordinances.
